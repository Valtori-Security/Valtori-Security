FAQ

Common questions

How does remote support work?

After checkout, Valtori Security contacts you using the phone number collected during checkout to schedule a remote session.

Is my data safe?

You stay present during the session and can close the remote connection at any time. Only share access after your appointment is confirmed.

What remote software is used?

AnyDesk or RustDesk can be used depending on the scheduled service and your system.

How long does service take?

Most services are completed in one remote session. Timing depends on system condition and plan scope.

What happens after payment?

The checkout provider should redirect you to the After Purchase page. Install remote support software there, then wait for Valtori Security to confirm your appointment.
